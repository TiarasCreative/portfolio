PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Opportunity


There is a clear opportunity to create a dedicated application for singers and vocal coaches that goes beyond the limited tools currently available. Many singers are unsure how to properly prepare their voice before a performance, what steps to take afterwards to safely warm down, or how to build long-term improvements in range, pitch, and projection.
A purpose-built platform could deliver daily guided warmups, cooldown routines, and gamified exercises, while also using reminders and streak-based motivation similar to Duolingo to encourage consistent practice. By combining expert content with engaging features, the app would provide real value to singers and teachers, supporting both performance readiness and long-term vocal development.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Mission


Our mission is to make vocal training accessible, engaging, and effective for singers of all levels. By combining guided warmups and warmdowns, real-time feedback, and daily goal setting with fun gamified exercises, we help singers build consistency, grow their range, improve projection, and protect their voices. Whether you are a beginner finding your voice or a professional preparing for performance, our goal is to provide the tools, reminders, and community that empower you to reach your full vocal potential every day.



Core Issues addressed


01 Daily Goals & Reminders: Users set practice goals and get nudges to keep consistent.

02 Warmups & Warmdowns: Guided routines for preparing before singing and cooling down after.

03 Scales & Suggestions: Easy exercises for beginners who don’t know what to practise.

04 Pitch Feedback: Real-time correction so singers can hear and see if they’re on key.

05 Breathing Exercises: Adjustable length to build projection and smoother notes.

06 Progress Tracking: Range and accuracy improve over time, with simple charts.

07 Gamification: Streaks, badges, and challenges to make practising fun.

08 Community & Teachers (Future): Option for coaches to assign exercises, and for singers to share progress.

The Impact


Since the launch of the project, the product has been downloaded by over 1000 users with that number steadily rising each day.

Happy customers

    04 75% of singers noticed clearer projection and smoother tone after regular breathing exercises.

Key Takeaways

  • Most apps focus on pitch correction and basic warmups, but lack comprehensive vocal training features.
  • There is a gap in tools for vocal health monitoring, projection training, and advanced techniques.
  • Few apps offer features for vocal coaches to manage students or assign exercises.
  • Gamification and community features are underutilized in vocal training apps.
  • Opportunities exist to integrate AI for more personalized feedback beyond pitch accuracy.
