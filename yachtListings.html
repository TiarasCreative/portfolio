PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Opportunity


The Global Yacht Charter Availability Calendar is a centralised platform connecting charter brokers and central agents through real-time yacht availability, verified listings, and direct inquiries. We’re on a mission to simplify and elevate the yacht charter process—especially across the Middle East and Asia—by offering seamless access to the region’s largest fleet. Calendars gives retail brokers instant visibility into live CA-managed calendars, ensures listing accuracy through a thorough verification process, and enables effortless communication between brokers and central agents.

DISCOVER
How does the elusive world of Luxury yachting actually work?

When a guest or VIP—known as a charterer—wants to book a yacht trip, they typically reach out to a charter broker with their preferences and desired dates.

The charter broker then searches for suitable yachts and presents a selection to the charterer. Once the charterer selects a yacht, the broker contacts the central agent responsible for managing that particular vessel.

The central agent, who represents the yacht owner, handles the confirmation and coordination of the booking, ensuring all details are managed smoothly on behalf of the owner.

DISCOVER

Broker Parity


I conducted a card sorting workshop with 8 brokers.


Key insights
  • Most participants aligned with the initial hypothesis.
  • Some felt the dashboard should be postponed until there is more clarity on the data requirements.
  • The majority believed that an in-app messaging feature might deter users, preferring a “copy central agent” email option instead.

NEXT STEPS

Reflection


As Product Design Lead for this application, I drove the end-to-end release to Open Beta and subsequent customer rollout

Went Well

Formative user research  was executed early and captured key insights from design leadership


Progress over perfection This project focused on a key value which was progress over perfection. We didn't aim to have a perfect platform built, we also didn't aim to have an endless amount of features.


Flexible components We designed the platform to respond to changes in the future. We future proofed alot of the components we used as we knew changes were inevitable. This has meant tweeks to our platform have been smooth.




To Improve

Brochure design  given the project experience again, I would have spent more time perfecting the brochure look and feel as this is the area where we receive the most customer complaints and requests.


In-app messaging: While brokers didn’t see this feature as a priority, it would have solved key problems we’re now facing. Users are copying agent details and leaving the platform, making it hard to track enquiries, show value to central agents, or report on performance. Without messaging happening on the platform, we lose visibility and can’t prove Ankor’s impact. Platforms like Airbnb use in-app messaging for exactly this reason—to protect data, track activity, and support the business.
